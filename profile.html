Bleached process

  • Raw textile materials in natural form, are known as greige material. This greige material will have its natural color, odor and impurities that are not suitable for clothing materials. The removal of these natural coloring matters and add-ons is known as bleaching.

Dyeing process

  • Dyeing is the process of adding color to textile materials like fabrics, fibers and yarns.
  • We carry out different kinds of fabric dyeing process like jigger dyeing, winch dyeing, soft flow dyeing, fabrics (cotton & viscose staple fiber) dyeing process to meet all export standards. We also execute dyed fabrics in zero zero finish and peach finish Etc.,.

Printing process

  • Textile printing is the process of applying beautiful colors to fabric in definite patterns or designs.
  • Our experts undertake rotary printing for fabrics (cotton & viscose staple fiber) in several types like pigment prints, reactive prints and discharge prints. All prints are done to match export standards.
